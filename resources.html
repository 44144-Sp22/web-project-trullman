FAQ:

Are all DnD books used?

     Every DM uses the Players Handbook, but it is up to them whether to allow more books for character creation and monsters.

What is experience and what does it mean?

     Experience is gained by fighting foes, or other situations is the DM allows it. To level up your character you must gain experience.

What is the coinage conversion?

  •      10 copper = 1 silver
  •      5 silver = 1 electrum
  •      10 silver = 1 gold
  •      10 gold = 1 platinum
